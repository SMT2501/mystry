What is M4?

MYSTRY 40UR is a lifestyle & media movement built on the 454 code:

  • 4Js – Jigga | Joint | Juice | Journey
  • 5Ps – Playa | Praya | Patience | Peace | Positivity
  • 4Gs – Game | God | Guap | Guttah

We fuse streetwear, visual storytelling, digital drops and a spiritual code to create real awareness in youth culture. The code is the foundation of everything we create. We rewrite stories through WORDS, PIX N MIX.

Why M4 Exists

We exist to remind people, especially the youth, that the beauty is in the unknown and awareness is the first step to freedom.

In a world full of noise, M4 is the signal — designed to awaken, inspire & guide the next leaders, creatives & hustlers.
